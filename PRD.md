# Product Requirements Document

## Project Name
MKS Ventures Website

## Objective
Launch a polished static website for MKS Ventures within 2 days that establishes a credible web presence for investors, customers, and partners.

## Business Goals
- Provide a clean, elegant online identity for a firm with minimal existing web presence.
- Communicate the company story, project experience, and current developments.
- Encourage visitors to contact the firm by phone.

## Audience
- Potential investors
- Business partners
- High-intent buyers or tenants

## Success Criteria
- Live static website on a new URL within 2 days.
- A working 3-page site with consistent navigation.
- Elegant, trustworthy visual design.
- Clear company story and project details.
- Prominent phone contact in the footer.
- Stakeholder approval from customers and investors.
