Who We Are

MKS Ventures is a Delhi-based real estate development firm with over 13 years of experience building residential and commercial spaces across the NCR region. We are a family-first business, now entering its second generation of entrepreneurs — carrying forward a legacy of craftsmanship, trust, and long-term thinking.

We build with relationships in mind. Every project we take on is a commitment — to the families who will call it home, the businesses that will grow within its walls, and the communities that surround it. Our work is grounded in sustainability, attention to detail, and a belief that great spaces outlast the people who build them.
